Outline

The stream will begin with an introduction of the members, where they will greet the audience, give an overview of the topic, and explain the purpose of the session. This opening segment will take around five minutes and will be shown through video with live audio. A short warm-up discussion will follow to engage the audience and prepare them for the main conversation. Afterward, the hosts will dive into the main discussion, spending about twenty minutes unpacking the key points of the topic. The session will then move into an interactive segment, allowing viewers to ask questions, seek clarifications, and share concerns in real time. Next, a brief reflection activity will be conducted to encourage viewers to appreciate and accept themselves. Finally, the stream will close with a five-minute segment thanking the audience for their participation and support.

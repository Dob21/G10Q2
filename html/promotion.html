Promotion

The promotional plan centers on using a playful, TikTok-inspired strategy where the group pretends to be well-known celebrities who have had a major influence on beauty standards. By adopting the personas of these iconic figures, the group creates a humorous yet eye-catching approach that immediately grabs attention on social media platforms. In each promotional clip, the “celebrities” greet viewers as if they’re recording a casual message for their fans. They introduce themselves using celebrity names and then proceed to invite the audience to join Group 1’s live stream. The message emphasizes that the stream will discuss beauty standards and their impact, particularly on women. This witty, character-based method not only aligns with current TikTok parody and impersonation trends but also helps deliver the invitation in a fun, relatable way that encourages female viewers to attend. By blending humor, celebrity culture, and relevant social issues, the promo plan becomes both entertaining and effective in attracting the target audience.
